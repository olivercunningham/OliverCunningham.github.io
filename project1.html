Assembly Line Throughput Predictor

Project Goal: Reduce the time required to generate quotes for new orders at an electronics manufacturing service provider.

Background

Quotes are generated to bid on contracts, however the process for generating quotes is labour and time intensive and consequently many potential contracts are lost due to an inability to produce timeley quotes.

The quoting process required typically upward up 6 hours of skilled labour to produce a quote for a new product.
